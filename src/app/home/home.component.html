AltruSight's Pledge

Giving back to givers: improving every charitable giver's ability to discover and connect to great causes.

  • Within the United States alone, over 300 billion dollars are donated to nonprofits.
  • Individual givers make up around 70% of the total donations, accounting for roughly $1,000 per individual.
  • As charitable giving continues to rise, givers need a way to track their donations and impact in a transparent way, find new causes, and connect with new givers and organizations.
  • Our goal is to place more power in the hands of the individual givers by connecting them with other givers and establishing a platform where transparent giving is the norm.
